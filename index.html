Privacy Policy for Classes X

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

Welcome to Classes X (package: com.gothwad.classesx), an educational application developed and managed by Gothwad Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless educational experience, we collect the following types of information based on your user role (Student, Parent, or Admin):

  • Personal Information: Name, mobile number/email address (used for OTP/authentication via Firebase).
  • Role-Specific Data:
    • Students: Enrolled batches, video watch history, test scores, and app usage activity.
    • Parents: Linked student profiles to track educational progress.
    • Admins: Internal management logs.

2. App Permissions Required

Classes X requires certain device permissions to function correctly. We only access these with your explicit consent:

  • Internet: Required to fetch live/recorded classes, sync data with our servers, and authenticate users.
  • Storage (Read/Write): Required to download, save, and access educational materials like PDF notes and encrypted offline videos securely on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively to improve your app experience:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To deliver video content and study materials seamlessly.
  • To allow parents to track their child's academic progress.
  • To ensure app security and manage access controls through our backend infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services (Backend)

We use Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Storage) as our primary backend provider to securely store user data, host video links/PDFs, and manage login sessions. Your data is encrypted and handled according to Google's strict security protocols.

5. Data Security

Gothwad Technologies implements robust security measures to protect your personal information. Content downloaded via the app (like videos and PDFs) may be stored in encrypted formats to prevent unauthorized sharing or access. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. User Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data. If you wish to delete your account or any associated data, you can do so by contacting our support team.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of any significant changes within the app.
